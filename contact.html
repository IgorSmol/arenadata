Our branch office in Asker is located in Dairy bend 2. This is right in the center of Asker, in the agro-farm. Input for parking going from Bankveien in the basement of Agro-farm, possibly entrance on foot inside the flit, a small pedestrian street inside the building.

Asker is about 2 mil from Oslo to Drammen. If you come by plane to Gardermoen Oslo Airport (OSL), then take the Airport Express Train or NSB trains to Asker station. Then there are 2 minutes to walk to our offices. PS. Not all Airport Express goes to Asker. Taxi from Gardermoen and to us costs about 850 million, and takes a little longer than the Airport Express Train, arrange a fixed price in advance. If you drive from Gardermoen, then take E6 towards Oslo, and take direction E18 west towards Drammen near Oslo.

Directions from Asker station. Follow Peat road that runs right out of the station. Turn left into Bank Road, then right into Dairy bend, which is across the road for Asker Fitness. Enter through the sliding doors to the right bakery. Over these it labeled "Dairy bend 2 - Input C". Alarm clock at the first door on the left, labeled "C" and "Arena Data." We are located on the 2nd floor.
